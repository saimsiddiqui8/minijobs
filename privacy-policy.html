Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2024

Welcome to MiniJobGermany.de. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: When you register on our site, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address.
  • Job Application Data: When you apply for jobs, we collect information related to your employment history, education, and other relevant data.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use our website, including your IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services:We use your information to process your job applications, respond to your inquiries, and improve our website's functionality.
  • To Communicate with You:We may use your email address to send you updates, job alerts, and other relevant information.
  • To Ensure Security:We use your information to protect our website and services from fraud and unauthorized access.

Sharing Your Information

  • With Employers:When you apply for jobs, we share your application details with the respective employers.
  • With Affiliate PartnersWe may share your information, including your resume and contact details, with our affiliate partners like Stetstone, Jooble, and other tech companies. These partners may contact you regarding job opportunities and other related services.
  • With Service Providers:We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing our services.
  • As Required by Law:We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement various security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

  • Access: You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to your personal data if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal obligations.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site and improve your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date at the top will be updated.
